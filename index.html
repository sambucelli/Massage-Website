About Me

Certified Massage Therapist Specializing in sports massage and pain management. I focus on helping clients overcome chronic, reoccurring pain. My practice centers around identifying and addressing the root causes of discomfort. Utilizing deep tissue techniques and targeting specific muscle groups, I work to manipulate stuck facia and cross fiber muscle tissue that is causing issues. I work closely with each client to alleviate tension, manage pain, and improve mobility, all while seeking to relieve trigger points and restore optimal function to the body. You can expect a personalized treatment plan to follow our first session. My goal is to provide lasting relief and support each client's journey to better physical health.
